Candidate Answers to East River Democratic Club Questionnaires

All Candidates were asked two questions:

1) What one policy would you address differently than the current occupant of the office you are seeking?

2) What is a cause that you've fought for in our districts (City Council 4 and 5 primarily) and how has it changed constituents' lives?


The East River Democratic Club Endorsed the following candidates for election in 2021:

Manhattan Borough President - Ben Kallos


Manhattan DA - Dan Quart


Comptroller - David Weprin


City Council 4 - Keith Powers


City Council 5 - Tricia Shimamura








The East River Democratic Club has not endorsed any candidate for Mayor
