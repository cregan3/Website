ABOUT EAST RIVER DEMOCRATIC CLUB

The East River Democratic Club is organized to help promote Democratic Values throughout New York City and State - specifically in the 76th Assembly District.

 

We are dedicated to providing a strong, honest, active, and progressive Democratic organization in the 76th Assembly District, New York County; To promote progressive policies and candidates in the Democratic Party at the local, state, and national levels; and To encourage the participation of all Democrats at the grassroots level in the affairs of their party and to encourage the enrollment in the Democratic Party of persons who subscribe to its principles.
